Why VISION?


Tradition since 1988

In its endeavor to provide a national platform for the youth to showcase their talents and skills in fierce competitions, displaying cutting edge technology and research from all over the globe we also have renowned personalities motivate the youth through keynote speeches.



Who We Are?

We started in 1988 with the motive of encouraging technology, scientific thinking and innovation. VISION is Science and Technology festival with a footfall of more than 13000 annually and a reach of over 120+ colleges.

How Is VISION Built?


Complete conceptualization and execution of Vision is done by students of in a three-tier team structure. We operate with a team of 21 Chief Coordinators, 40 Coordinators and around 100 volunteers. The team primarily include tasks like event designing, accounts, infrastructure, media and marketing, hospitality, publicity, web and creatives.
